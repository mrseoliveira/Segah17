Call for Papers

Prospective authors are invited to submit original technical papers and posters for publication in the SeGAH 2017 Conference Proceedings and for presentation in the symposia.

Full Papers

Prospective authors are invited to submit their papers to SeGAH 2017, according to the following guidelines:

  • doneThe length of each paper, including figures and references, must range between 4‑8 pages and should conform to the IEEE CS proceedings style - Column Format
  • doneAll papers must be submitted in PDF format, using EasyChair submission system
  • doneSubmissions should include paper title, name of authors, their affiliations, email addresses and an abstract
  • doneSubmissions should explicitly state their contribution and their relevance to the theme of the conference
  • doneOther criteria for selection will be originality, significance, correctness, and clarity
  • info_outlineSimultaneous or similar submissions to other conferences or journals are not allowed

Please note that at least one author of each accepted paper is required to:

  • doneRegister for the conference at the full rate
  • doneAttend the conference and present the paper

Tutorials

The role of the tutorials is to provide a platform for a more intensive scientific exchange amongst researchers interested in a particular topic and as a meeting point for the community. Tutorials complement the depth-oriented technical sessions by providing participants with broad overviews of emerging fields. Academics can schedule a tutorial for 1 hour. If you wish to propose a Tutorial, please kindly send to the secretariat (secretariat@segah.org) an expression of interest in order to obtain further information about how to submit a formal proposal. We will be happy to send you appropriate guidelines.

Demos

Demo sessions aim to create an environment where companies are encouraged to interactively demonstrate developed tools, systems and products in the scope of SEGAH. Exhibitors from industrial partners, are welcome and can schedule a Demo for half-day, and the organizers are expected to manage the all the event. If you wish to propose a new Demo, please kindly send to the secretariat (secretariat@segah.org) an expression of interest in order to obtain further information about how to submit a formal proposal. We will be happy to send you appropriate guidelines.

Poster Presentation

Display Area:

  • 59,4 cm x 84,1 cm (DIN A1 format)
  • Thumb tacks will be provided to hang your poster

Format:

  • Posters presentations are hard-copy (paper/poster) format only
  • Posters must fit within the designated space listed above
  • The most successful and attractive posters are graphically-produced posters that highlight and summarize the main points, with the poster presenter filling in the details
  • The least effective poster format is a photocopy of the manuscript.

Your poster presentation should include:

  • The paper title and all authors at the top of the poster
  • A brief introduction, goals, experimental detail, conclusions, and references
  • Explanations for graphs, pictures, and tables.

Font Sizes (minimum recommendations):

  • Title: 36 point type
  • List of authors: 25 point type
  • Double-spaced text: 15 point type
  • The entire poster layout should be readable from six to ten feet away
  • Be careful in your use of colors, some people are red-green color blind and some colors do not stand out in contrast to others.
