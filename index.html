Welcome to the 5th Conference on Serious Games and Applications for Health

 

It is with great pleasure that we invite you to participate in the 5th International Conference on Serious Games and Applications for Health, SeGAH 2017, to be held in Perth, Western Australia, from 2-4 April 2017, at the Perth Concention and Exhibition Center, co-located with 26th International World Wide Web Conference, 2017.

Important Dates
Full Paper Submission: October 30, 2016
Short Paper Submission: October 30, 2016
Tutorial Submission: October 30, 2016
Demo Submission: October 30, 2016
Special-Topic Workshop Submission: October 30, 2016
Authors Notification: December 7, 2016
Final Paper Submission and Registration: January 13, 2017
Early Registration: January 13, 2017

Scope

The overall objectives of the conference are the discussion and sharing of knowledge, experiences and scientific and technical results, related to state-of-the-art solutions and technologies on serious games and applications for health and healthcare, as well as the demonstration of advanced products and technologies.more_vert

Scopeclose

The overall objectives of the conference are the discussion and sharing of knowledge, experiences and scientific and technical results, related to state-of-the-art solutions and technologies on serious games and applications for health and healthcare, as well as the demonstration of advanced products and technologies.

Best paper awards will be distributed during the conference closing session.

Topics

Submitted papers will be subject to a blind review process. All accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings and will be available at the IEEE Xplorer. The Proceedings will be submitted for indexation by: Thomson Reuters Conference Proceedings Citation Index; DBLP Computer Science Bibliography; and Inspec.
